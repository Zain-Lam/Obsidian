# semantics for conjunction symbol

TD: YEAR1::TERM2::PH136 Logic 1
Q: What are the semantics for the $\land$ symbol
?
A:$(\phi \land \psi)$ is _iff_ both $\phi$ and $\psi$ are both true.
$$
\begin{array}{c|c||c}
\phi & \psi & (\phi \land \psi) \\
\hline
T & T & T \\
T & F & F \\
F & T & F \\
F & F & F
\end{array}
$$
