# semantics for the material conditional symbol

TD: YEAR1::TERM2::PH136 Logic 1
Q: What are the semantics for $(\phi \to \psi)$?
?
A:$(\phi \to \psi)$ is true _iff_ $\phi$ is false and/or $\psi$ is true.
$$
\begin{array}{c|c||c}
\phi & \psi & (\phi \to \psi) \\
\hline
T & T & T \\
T & F & F \\
F & T & T \\
F & F & T
\end{array}
$$
