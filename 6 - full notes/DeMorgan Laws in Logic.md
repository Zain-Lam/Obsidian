# DeMorgan Laws in Logic

TD: YEAR1::TERM2::PH136 Logic 1
Q: State DeMorgan's Laws
A: $\lnot(\phi \lor \psi)\Leftrightarrow(\lnot\phi \land \lnot\psi)$ and $\lnot(\phi \land \psi)\Leftrightarrow(\lnot\phi \lor \lnot\psi)$
