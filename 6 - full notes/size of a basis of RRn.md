# size of a basis of RRn

TD: YEAR1::TERM2::MA150 Algebra 2::03 Subspaces and Bases of R^n 
Q: Let $S \subset \mathbb{R}^n$ be a basis. Then what can be said about the size of $S$?
A: $S$ is a finite set and $\#S = n$.
