# inverse of a 2x2 matrix

TD: YEAR1::TERM2::MA150 Algebra 2::02 Linear Systems and matrices 
Q: For a matrix $$
\begin{pmatrix}
a & b  \\
c & d
\end{pmatrix}
$$ what is the inverse?
A: When $ad-bc\neq0$ the inverse is given by $$
\frac{1}{ad - bc} \begin{pmatrix} d & -b \\ -c & a \end{pmatrix}
$$
