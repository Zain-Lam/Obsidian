# prove that exp(p) = e^p

TD: YEAR1::TERM2::MA139 Analysis 2::01 Power Series 1
Q: Prove that $\exp(p) = e^p$ $\forall$ $p \in \mathbb{R}$
A: By definition of the power,
$$
e^p = \exp(p \log(e)).
$$
Now we know that $\log(e) = 1$, so the second expression is $\exp(p)$.
