# GL2R definition

TD: YEAR1::TERM1::MA151 Algebra 1::03 Groups
Q: how do we define $GL_{2}\mathbb{R}$?
A: $$GL_{2}\mathbb{R}=\left\{\begin{pmatrix}  a & b\\  c & d \end{pmatrix}|a,b,c,d\in\mathbb{R} \text{ and }ad-bc \neq 0\right\}$$
