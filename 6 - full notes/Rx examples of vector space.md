Q: How is the ring of all polynomials (in $x$ and with real coefficients) denoted?
A: $\mathbb{R}[x] = \{ a_0 + a_1 x + a_2 x^2 + \ldots + a_s x^s \mid s \in \mathbb{N} \text{ and all } a_i \in \mathbb{R} \}$
