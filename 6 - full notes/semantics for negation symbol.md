# semantics for negation symbol

TD: YEAR1::TERM2::PH136 Logic 1
Q: What are the semantics for the $\lnot$ symbol
A: $\lnot\phi$ is true _iff_ $\phi$ is false.
$$\begin{array}{|c|c|}
\phi & \lnot\phi \\
\hline 
T & F\\
F & T\\
\end{array}
$$
