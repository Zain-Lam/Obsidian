# logarithm definition proof

TD: YEAR1::TERM2::MA139 Analysis 2::01 Power Series 1
Q: Prove that $\log(uv) = \log u + \log v$.
A: $$
e^{\log u + \log v} = e^{\log u} e^{\log v} = uv.
$$and then apply log to both sides to get what we want.
