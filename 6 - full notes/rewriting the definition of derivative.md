# rewriting the definition of derivative

TD: YEAR1::TERM2::MA139 Analysis 2::02 Limits and the derivative
Q: How do we rewrite the derivative at c
A: $$
f'(c) = \lim_{x \to c} \frac{f(x) - f(c)}{x - c}
$$
