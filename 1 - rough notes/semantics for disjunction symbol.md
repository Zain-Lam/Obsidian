# semantics for disjunction symbol

TD: YEAR1::TERM2::PH136 Logic 1
Q: What are the semantics for the $\lor$ symbol?
A: $(\phi \lor \psi)$ is true _iff_ $\phi$ and/or $\psi$ are true
$$
\begin{array}{c|c||c}
\phi & \psi & (\phi \lor \psi) \\
\hline
T & T & T \\
T & F & T \\
F & T & T \\
F & F & F
\end{array}
$$
