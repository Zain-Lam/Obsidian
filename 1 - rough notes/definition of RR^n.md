# definition of RR^n

TD: YEAR1::TERM2::MA150 Algebra 2::01 Column Vectors in Real^n 
Q: define $\mathbb{R}^{n}$
A: $$
\mathbb{R}^n = \left\{ 
\left.\begin{pmatrix} 
a_1 \\ 
a_2 \\ 
\vdots \\ 
a_n 
\end{pmatrix} 
\,\right|\, 
a_i \in \mathbb{R} \text{ for all } i = 1, \dots, n 
\right\}
$$This is called __$n$-space__.
