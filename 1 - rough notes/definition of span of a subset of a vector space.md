# definition of span of a subset of a vector space

TD: YEAR1::TERM2::MA150 Algebra 2::04 Vector spaces 
Q: If $S \subset V$ is a non-empty subset of a vector space $V$. What is the span of $S$, denoted $\langle S \rangle$?
A: $\langle S \rangle = \{ \lambda_1 \underline{\mathbf{v}}_1 + \ldots + \lambda_s \underline{\mathbf{v}}_s \mid \underline{\mathbf{v}}_1, \ldots, \underline{\mathbf{v}}_s \in S \text{ and } \lambda_1, \ldots, \lambda_s \in \mathbb{R} \}$
And by convention, the span of the empty subset $S = \emptyset \subset V$ to be $\langle \emptyset \rangle = \{0_V\}$.
