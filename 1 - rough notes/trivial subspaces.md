# trivial subspaces

TD: YEAR1::TERM2::MA150 Algebra 2::03 Subspaces and Bases of R^n 
Q: What are the two trivial subspaces of $\mathbb{R}^{n}$
A: The two trivial subspaces are:
$\{0\} \subset \mathbb{R}^n$ and $\mathbb{R}^n \subset \mathbb{R}^n$.
