# derivative of log

TD: YEAR1::TERM2::MA139 Analysis 2::01 Power Series 1
Q: Find the derivative of $x \to \log x$
A: Let $f$ be the exponential function and $g$ the logarithm. Then$$ g'(x) = \frac{1}{f'(g(x))} = \frac{1}{\exp(\log x)} = \frac{1}{x}. $$
